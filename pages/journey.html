I am Max

I was born in Odesa, Ukraine, and grew up around basketball. At 14, I got a scholarship to play in California, which eventually took me across the US - Kansas, Michigan, Ohio, Texas and 20+ other states where I spend nearly seven years chasing the game.

After graduating in 2023, I interned for We Are D3, a professional basketball team competing in the $1 million TBT tournament. I began there as intern who helped with every tasks I was given but also seeing how data quietly drives decisions behind the scenes — strategy, scouting, performance - got me genuinely curious about that side of things.

That fall I went to play college basketball at GCC in Michigan, where I also wrote my first lines of code. Studying Computer Programming alongside basketball, I got my first taste of IT support and started getting serious about tech.

In the summer of 2024 I rejoined We Are D3 and traveled with the team to Dayton, Ohio, where I watched them win their first tournament in six years.

That summer - between Ohio and traveling around New England - made it clear what I wanted to do next. I decided to leave basketball behind and focus on data and AI full time.

I moved to Aberdeen, Scotland to study Data Science with Business Management at Robert Gordon University. Still play basketball here, but the focus now is on how AI and data can actually help people make better decisions.
